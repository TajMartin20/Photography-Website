Aperture

Aperture (f-stop) controls how much light enters and depth of field. Lower f-numbers (f/1.8) = wide aperture, shallow DOF; higher f-numbers (f/11) = deep DOF.

Creative use

  • Portraits: wide aperture for subject isolation.
  • Landscapes: small aperture for greater sharpness across the scene.
