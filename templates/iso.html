ISO

ISO controls the sensor's sensitivity to light. Lower ISO (100–200) = cleaner images, higher ISO (1600+) = more noise but better low-light performance.

Practical tips

  • Use base ISO when possible for best image quality.
  • Increase ISO to maintain shutter speed/aperture in low light.
  • Use noise reduction in post when necessary.
