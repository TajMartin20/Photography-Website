Shutter Speed

Shutter speed controls how long the sensor is exposed. Fast speeds (1/1000s) freeze motion; slow speeds (1/30s, 1s+) create motion blur or light trails.

Guidelines

  • Handheld rule: use shutter >= 1/(focal length) to avoid camera shake.
  • Use tripod for long exposures and night photography.
