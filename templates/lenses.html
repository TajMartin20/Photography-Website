Lenses

Focal length determines field of view. Wide-angle (<35mm) captures more scene, standard (~50mm) approximates human sight, telephoto (>85mm) magnifies distant subjects.

Prime vs Zoom

Primes have wider apertures and typically sharper images. Zooms give framing flexibility.

When to choose

  • Portraits: 50mm–135mm
  • Landscape: 10–35mm
  • Sports/Wildlife: 200mm+
